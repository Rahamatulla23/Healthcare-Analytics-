# Healthcare Analytics & Hospital Management Dashboard (Power BI)

This project is a comprehensive Power BI dashboard built to analyze hospital performance, patient demographics, disease trends, financial metrics, and doctor efficiency.

## 🔧 Requirements
- Microsoft Power BI Desktop
- Dataset: Healthcare Analytics (patients, doctors, appointments, revenue, payment methods, diseases, cities)
- Fields used: 
  - patient_id, patient_name, age, gender, city, disease, admission_date  
  - doctor_id, doctor_name, specialization, experience_years  
  - appointment_id, consultation_fee, treatment_cost, payment_method  
  - Total Revenue, Total Appointments

## 📊 Visuals Included
1. **KPIs (Cards):**
   - Total Appointments
   - Total Patients
   - Total Doctors
   - Total Revenue

2. **Disease Analysis:**
   - Bar Chart → Most Common Diseases
   - Matrix → Disease vs Gender vs Revenue

3. **Demographics:**
   - Pie Chart → Male vs Female Patient Ratio

4. **Financial Insights:**
   - Line Chart → Revenue Trend Over Time (Monthly)
   - Area Chart → Busy Months in Hospital

5. **Payment Insights:**
   - Donut Chart → Most Used Payment Mode (Cash, Card, UPI, Insurance)

6. **Doctor Performance:**
   - Bar Chart → Most Consulted Doctors
   - Scatter Plot → Experience vs Earnings

7. **Geographic Insights:**
   - Map Visual → Cities with High Patient Visits

## 🎯 Key Insights
- Fever and Migraine generated the highest revenue overall.
- Female patients contributed more revenue compared to males.
- March was the busiest month (59 visits), while May was the least busy (9 visits).
- Insurance, Cash, Card, and UPI were almost evenly used as payment methods.
- Experienced doctors showed higher earnings, with Dr. Jeffrey and Dr. John leading in consultations.
- Cities like Hyderabad, Bangalore, Chennai, and Mumbai had the highest patient visits.
